Gryffindor

Founded by Godric Gryffindor

Gryffindor values bravery, courage, chivalry, and daring. The lion represents the house, and its colors are red and gold. Famous members include Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, and Ron Weasley. Gryffindors are known for standing up for what’s right, even when it’s difficult.

Slytherin

Founded by Salazar Slytherin

Slytherin house values ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness. Represented by a serpent, its colors are green and silver. Notable members include Severus Snape and Draco Malfoy. Slytherins are strategic thinkers and often seek greatness at any cost.

Ravenclaw

Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw house celebrates intelligence, wisdom, creativity, and wit. Its emblem is an eagle, and the colors are blue and bronze. Famous Ravenclaws include Luna Lovegood and Cho Chang. Ravenclaws prize learning and original thinking.

Hufflepuff

Founded by Helga Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff house values hard work, loyalty, patience, and fairness. Represented by a badger, its colors are yellow and black. Notable Hufflepuffs include Cedric Diggory and Nymphadora Tonks. Hufflepuffs are known for their kindness and strong moral compass.

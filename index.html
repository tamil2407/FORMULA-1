RED BULL RACING

Founded by Dietrich Mateschitz | Drivers: Max Verstappen, Yuki Tsunoda

Red Bull Racing (officially Oracle Red Bull Racing) is a top-tier Formula 1 team owned by Red Bull GmbH. Based in Milton Keynes, UK, the team is known for multiple Drivers’ and Constructors’ Championships, cutting-edge aerodynamics, and dominance under Max Verstappen.

History of Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing began in 2005 after Dietrich Mateschitz purchased the Jaguar F1 team. The team rose rapidly, dominating the sport from 2010–2013 with Sebastian Vettel and again from 2021 onward with Max Verstappen.

Early Years & Foundations (2005–2008)

Debut: 2005 Australian Grand Prix

Drivers: David Coulthard, Christian Klien

Team Principal: Christian Horner

The Vettel Era & Dominance (2009–2013)

Sebastian Vettel led Red Bull to four consecutive Drivers’ and Constructors’ Championships, establishing one of the most dominant eras in Formula 1 history.

Modern Era (2014–Present)

After early hybrid-era struggles, Red Bull partnered with Honda and returned to dominance. Max Verstappen became the face of modern Formula 1 success.

Key Figures

Dietrich Mateschitz & Helmut Marko – Founders & Visionaries

Christian Horner – Team Principal

Sebastian Vettel & Max Verstappen – Championship Drivers

MERCEDES-AMG PETRONAS

Drivers: George Russell, Andrea Kimi Antonelli

Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS Formula One Team is one of the most successful teams in F1 history, dominating the hybrid era from 2014 to 2020 with record-breaking achievements.

Key Aspects

Base: Brackley & Brixworth, UK

Ownership: Mercedes-Benz Group, INEOS, Toto Wolff

Technology: Advanced hybrid power units and aerodynamics

McLaren Formula 1

Drivers: Lando Norris, Oscar Piastri

McLaren is one of Formula 1’s most historic teams, known for innovation, iconic drivers like Senna and Hamilton, and its famous papaya orange livery.

Scuderia Ferrari

Drivers: Charles Leclerc, Lewis Hamilton

Ferrari is the oldest and most iconic team in Formula 1 history. Based in Maranello, Italy, Ferrari represents passion, heritage, and record-breaking championship success.
